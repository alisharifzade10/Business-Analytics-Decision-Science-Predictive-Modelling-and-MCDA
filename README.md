Business Analytics & Decision Science
Predictive Modelling & Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis

This project applies business analytics and decision science methods to solve two strategic business problems:

Revenue prediction in e-commerce

Multi-criteria robot selection for logistics operations

🔹 1. Revenue Prediction – SweetAroma (E-commerce)

Objective:
Analyse how customer characteristics and on-site behaviour influence revenue and build a predictive model to estimate spending.

Data Includes:

Customer history

Website engagement metrics

Promotional activity

Transaction data (July 2025)

Methods Used:

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Regression modelling

Model validation using RMSE

Out-of-sample testing on 20 future orders

Business Value:

Identifies high-value customers

Supports targeted marketing strategies

Enables revenue forecasting

🔹 2. Robot Selection – AutonomousShipment (MCDA)

Objective:
Select the most suitable autonomous delivery robot among seven prototypes under multiple conflicting criteria.

Alternatives:
Alpha, Bravo, Charlie, Delta, Echo, Foxtrot, Gamma

Evaluation Criteria:

Operational performance

Cost efficiency

Reliability

Technical specifications

Methods Used:

Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis (MCDA)

Weighted Sum Model (WSM)

Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP)

Business Value:

Structured decision framework

Transparent ranking of alternatives

Evidence-based technology selection
